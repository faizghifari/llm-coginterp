# Morality, Ethics, and Values

This document categorizes benchmarks related to morality, ethical reasoning, and value alignment.

## Morality/Ethics Benchmarks

### AITA Normative (`aita-normative`) [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.18081)
**Subcategory:** normative-reasoning

Evaluates LLM moral judgment using real AITA (Am I The Asshole) scenarios.

### Moral Machine Cross-Language (`MoralMachine`) [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.02273)
**Subcategory:** cross-cultural-morality

Tests Trolley Problem-style moral dilemmas across languages; probes cultural variation in moral preferences.

### Moral Machine for LLMs (`moral-machine-llms`) [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.02273)
**Subcategory:** moral-dilemma

Applies the Moral Machine experiment to LLMs; evaluates utilitarian vs. deontological trade-offs.
