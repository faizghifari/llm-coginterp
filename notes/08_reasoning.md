### CriticBench (`criticbench`) [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.14809)
**Subcategory:** critique-correction

General reasoning benchmark evaluating LLMs' ability to generate, critique, and correct their own reasoning across 5 domains: Math (GSM8K), Commonsense (CSQA), Algorithmic, Coding (MBPP/HumanEval), Symbolic (BIG-Bench). 3,800 instances from 15 datasets. Uses F1 for critique accuracy, accuracy for generation/correction. 12 models evaluated. Batch 8 addition.
